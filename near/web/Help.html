Music

Command Description / Help
--connect Connect to a Voice Channel
--disconnect Disconnect bot from Voice Channel
--play <song-name/link> Play a song. Searches YouTube. Only YouTube links are supported
--skip Skip the currently playing song
--pause Pause the music
--resume Resume the music
--seek <seconds> Skip the given seconds of the playing song
--volume <number> Change the volume of the song
--loop <type> Play music in a loop
--nowplaying Show the song which is being played right now
--queue Display the songs waiting to be played
--equalizer Maybe tune the song to your liking?

Frequently Asked Questions

A list of most common questions asked about NearBot

What is the bot prefix?

The prefix of NearBot is --

Use --help to show the list of all the commands in discord

Can this Discord bot play music?

Yes you can play music by entering the name of the song with the relevant commands or by entering the YouTube video link. This bot supports only YouTube media. All music commands are still in BETA. If you find any issue, make sure to report the it from here
